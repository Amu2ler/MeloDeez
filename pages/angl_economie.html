Deezer economics

Economic system

Deezer is a music streaming platform that generates revenue primarily from two sources: paid subscriptions and advertising. Users can access music free of charge with advertisements, or take out subscriptions for an ad-free experience with additional features.

Objectifs

In 2023, Deezer aims to attract new subscribers and retain its existing base by strengthening its platform with new features. The aim is to remain competitive with other music streaming services. Deezer also wants to intensify engagement between artists and their audiences, explore new revenue sources, develop its B2B partnerships, control costs and aim for sales growth of over 10% while reducing its financial losses.
