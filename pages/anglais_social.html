We had the privilege of interviewing a Deezer employee during a video conference. Discover their responses to the questions below:

Question 1: Can you tell us about your professional journey at Deezer and what led you to join this company?

My professional journey at Deezer began after completing my computer science program in Paris. I joined the company as a junior developer, where I had the opportunity to work on various exciting projects. I have always been fascinated by music and technology, making Deezer the ideal choice for me.

Question 2: What qualities do you appreciate most in your colleagues and what are your expectations of them?

I appreciate collaboration and good teamwork within our team. For me, efficiency and rigor are essential qualities. I expect my colleagues to share these values and work together to achieve our common goals.

Question 3: How has the COVID-19 pandemic influenced your work mode at Deezer and your relationships with your colleagues?

The COVID-19 crisis has been effectively managed through remote work, allowing our team to remain productive. The relationship with my colleagues is excellent, even at a distance. We have maintained constant communication and solved problems together.

Question 4: As an aspiring project manager, what are your future goals at Deezer?

My primary goal is to advance and become a project manager. I want to contribute more to project management and the company's direction. I am determined to continue developing my management and leadership skills to achieve this goal.

Question 5: "Speed sometimes creates problems, but rigor is very useful." How do you apply this philosophy in your work at Deezer?

This philosophy resonates with me. My speed can sometimes lead to errors, which is why I strive to be rigorous in my tasks. I place great importance on precision and organization, which is essential to ensure the quality of our work at Deezer.

Question 6: Can you tell us about the challenges you face in your daily role at Deezer?

As a developer, challenges often involve solving complex technical problems and managing tight deadlines. Additionally, I am working on improving my management skills to progress to a project manager role. This involves juggling between technical development and management responsibilities, which can be demanding but rewarding.
