Other Deezer services

Flow

The feature that gets to know you, plays and recommends songs over and over again based on your listening preferences

Flow Moods

a Flow extension that lets you choose a particular mood and then generates an infinite playlist based on that mood

SongCatcher

is an application designed by Deezer to help you identify the music you hear around you. Launch SongCatcher and it will recognize the music you're listening to and give you information such as title and singer.
You can even hum or whistle the song you want to know about, and SongCatcher will recognize it.

Zen

is an application designed by Deezer offering a special selection of playlists, guided meditations, nature sounds and soft music designed to promote relaxation, concentration and sleep

.
