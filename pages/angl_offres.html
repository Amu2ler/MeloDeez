Others Deezer services

Flow

the feature that gets to know you, plays and recommends songs endlessly based on your listening preferences

Flow Moods

a Flow extension that lets you choose a particular mood and then generates an infinite playlist based on that mood

SongCatcher

is an application designed by Deezer helping you identify the music you hear around you. Launch SongCatcher and it will recognize the music you are listening to and give you its information such as the title and singer.
You can even hum the song you would like to know the information about or whistle it and SongCatcher will recognize it

Zen

is an application designed by Deezer offering a special selection of playlists, guided meditations, nature sounds and soft music designed to promote relaxation, concentration and sleep





BlindTest


Challenge your music knowledge! guess the title of the song too quickly as possible by typing the answer within a given time limit. The Deezer Blind Test generally offers a wide variety of music genres and artists. You can test your knowledge on a wide range of musical pieces.
